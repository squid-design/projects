Work With Us

Analytical Flavor Systems builds quality control tools for artisan beverage producers. Our tools track and flag products for batch variation, contamination, or latent flaws. We protect our clients from shipping a bad batch or a product that doesn't meet their quality standards. We are an angel-backed, early-stage startup with clients and revenue. We consider ourselves a high-performance team with an unshakable work ethic.

As an employee of Analytical Flavor Systems, you will be trained as a professional taster and learn the production processes of our clients.

For further information, please check out our Whitepapers and Open Source page.
