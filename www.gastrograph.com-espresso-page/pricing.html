    How much does Gastrograph cost?

    The price depends on your current batch size, among other factors. Drop us a line, and we’ll get you a customized quote right away.

    What are my payment options?

    We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, JCB, and Diners Club credit/debit cards as well as ACH transfer payments online. Want to pay via invoice? Sure! We'll gladly set up an annual invoice, just call or contact us.

    Do we have to pay for each user?

    No. This is an enterprise-wide license, with no hidden charges based on usage. We find the all-inclusive pricing model works better for our customers, and makes budgeting a breeze.

    How long does it take to get up and running?

    Most companies have a fully functional quality control program within two to four weeks. The exact time depends on the integration approach you choose, and how quickly your team completes the task. We recommend budgeting at least four weeks.

    Will third parties have access to my data?

    Only data which you explicitly authorize and send to a third-party will ever leave Gastrograph. We don’t sell customer data to anyone.

    Can I speak to an expert when Gastrograph flags a problem with my product?

    Yes! All plans come with world class email support - you'll receive the help you need within 24 hours. Enterprise clients can purchase premium support to connect with an expert on our data or chemistry team by phone at any time.

    Are there extra support or training packages I can buy?

    Personalized training and business-class support are free to all our customers. Need additional help with something? Your Account Manager will be there to provide it.

    Technical Training

    We start by doing a webinar with your quality control and production team (or the managers depending on team size and needs) where we run through Gastrograph’s features, reports, and data needs. We'll also show you how to configure your Quality Control alert settings to decide who receives a call, text, or email when there’s a problem. Your Gastrograph Account Manager will make sure that we’re flagging flaws, taints, contaminations, and batch variations from day one.

    Tasting Training

    Gastrograph Review for iOS and Android includes integrated lifecycle training customized for the sensitivity and demographic of each unique reviewer. By analyzing tens of thousands of reviews on thousands of unique products, we’re able to help each reviewer individually become more sensitive and knowledgeable about what they taste, and their own preferences.

    No long-term contract

    Upgrade, downgrade, pause, or cancel your plan at any time. Your success is our goal.

    Bank Grade Security

    We take the security of your data seriously. Your flavor profiles, quality metrics, and analysis are competitive information. We keep your data safe using HTTPS and bank-grade encryption.
