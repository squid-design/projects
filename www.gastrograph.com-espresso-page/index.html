Acetaldehyde/acetal

Tastes like: Aldehydic, bruised/green apples, emulsion paint.

Caused by: High oxygen levels in packaged beer, bacterial contamination (especially acetic acid bacteria), PET production problems, and/or incomplete fermentation.

Acetic acid

Tastes like: Vinegar (especially white vinegar).

Caused by: Fermentation problems, wild yeast, bacterial infection (especially acetic acid bacteria), and/or other microbiological contaminants.

Butyric acid

Tastes like: Putrid/rancid, baby vomit, acrid.

Caused by: Bacterial infection during mashing, wort separation and/or after packaging.

Caprylic acid

Tastes like: Goaty, waxy, fatty, tallowy.

Caused by: Beer ageing (conditioning), fermentation problems, and/or spoiled hops.

p-menthane-8-thiol-3-one

Tastes like: Catty, blackcurrant leaves, ribes, tom-cat urine.

Caused by: Early exposure to oxidation and/or high temperatures during storage and/or spoiled hops.

Diacetyl

Tastes like: Buttery, butterscotch, milky.

Caused by: Natural byproduct of yeast during fermentation. Can also be caused by a bacterial contamination.

Dimethyl sulfide (DMS)

Tastes like: Sweet corn, creamed corn, cooked vegetables, tomato sauce, sea vegetables.

Caused by: Malt-derived precursor during wort production and/or bacterial contamination.

Ethyl butyrate

Tastes like: Tropical fruits, mango, tinned pineapple.

Caused by: Byproduct of fermentation and/or butyric acid in wort.

Sour organic acids

Tastes like: Sour.

Caused by: Bacterial contamination.

Ethyl Hexanoate

Tastes like: Estery, apple, anise.

Caused by: Natural byproduct of fermentation.

cis-3-hexenol

Tastes like: Leafy, grassy, crushed green leaves, hedge clippings.

Caused by: Wort boiling problems, natural flavor of some hops, and/or ageing.

Isobutyraldehyde

Tastes like: Grainy, nuts.

Caused by: Insufficient wort boiling and/or poor yeast condition.

4-vinyl guaiacol

Tastes like: Spicy, herbal, clove-like.

Caused by: Presence of wild yeast during fermentation, bacterial contamination, and/or phenolic contamination of raw materials.

Hydrogen sulfide (H2S)

Tastes like: Sulphur, rotten eggs.

Caused by: Byproduct of yeast during fermentation and/or maturation and/or bacterial contamination.

Oxidized hop oils

Tastes like: Hop oil.

Caused by: Improper addition of hops.

Isoamyl Acetate

Tastes like: Estery, fruity, bananas, pear drops (candy).

Caused by: Byproduct of yeast during fermentation.

3-methyl-2-butene-1-thiol

Tastes like: Skunky, sunstruck.

Caused by: Exposure of finished beer to sunlight and/or artificial light.

Mercaptan (ethanethiol)

Tastes like: Sink drain, rotten vegetables, leek-like, natural gas.

Caused by: Byproduct of yeast during fermentation and/or yeast autolysis during maturation.

Metallic ion taints

Tastes like: Metallic.

Caused by: Tainted raw ingredients (especially barley) and/or poor quality metal surfaces of equipment.

Trans-2-nonenal

Tastes like: Cardboard, oxidized.

Caused by: Oxidation and/or ageing (mainly due to storage conditions, temperature, and amount of available oxygen in the packaged beer).

How It Works


The Gastrograph App for Android and iOS collects a wealth of information including demographic, situational, environmental, and reviewer data. It then sends this information to our secure, cloud-based Producer Panel after every review. You will be notified of variations between batches, possible contaminations, and/or flaws that our state-of-the-art data algorithms detect.

The Need for Quality

Producing a consistently high‐quality product is hard. Between flaws, taints, contaminations, and batch‐to‐batch deviations, every batch has limitless combinations of problems that could hurt your brand and lose your customer's loyalty. The craft beverage industry (beer, coffee, spirits, and wine) is one of the fastest growing markets in the world — demand has never been higher and the need for consistent quality has never been more important. Every batch you produce is a chance to turn someone into a brand loyalist, grow your market share, and build your company for the future.

The Gastrograph Solution

Gastrograph is a set of tools that makes quality control easy and painless for producers of artisan beverages. Your team reviews your products while Gastrograph provides real-time analytics and information on the flavor profile of every batch. You'll be able to not only make better decisions on how to ship, store, and optimize the flavor profile of every product you produce, but also compare profiles between batches and individual products, analyze your consumers' preferences, and most importantly, ensure the long-term quality of your product.

We help our customers get the answers they're looking for -
and we do it quicker, easier, cheaper, and more precisely than they could on their own.

The Gastrograph system is based on sensory science, data mining, and analytical chemistry. The review app for iOS and Android sends over 600 variables to our servers, where we run proprietary machine learning and artificial intelligence models in the cloud 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, giving you real-time, constant vigilance over changes in your products flavor profile and alerting you to quality control risks.
